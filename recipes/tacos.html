Tacos

Delicious tacos with a flour based tortilla

The recipe is easy and fast to make and is very healthy aswell! It has a lot of vegetables and is easy on the stomache

You also don't need a lot of supplies and the dishes will be held to a minimum

  • Beans
  • 500g minced beef
  • Tortilla
  • Seasoning
  • Salsa
  1. Put the minced beef in a frying pan and bake it untill it is not raw anymore
  2. Put the Seasoning and the beans with the meat and stir well
  3. Heat the tortilla's in a pan for 20 seconds
  4. Scoop some of the mixed meat into the torilla and dress it with some salsa

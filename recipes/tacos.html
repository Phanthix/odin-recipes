Tacos

Delicious tacos with a flour based tortilla

The recipe is easy and fast to make and is very healthy aswell! It has a lot of vegetables and is easy on the stomache

You also don't need a lot of supplies and the dishes will be held to a minimum

    • Beans
      500g minced beef
      Tortilla
      Seasoning
      Salsa
    • Put the minced beef in a frying pan and bake it untill it is not raw anymore
      Put the Seasoning and the beans with the meat and stir well
      Heat the tortilla's in a pan for 20 seconds
      Scoop some of the mixed meat into the torilla and dress it with some salsa

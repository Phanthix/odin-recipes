Pasta bolognese

Easy and delicious meal

This meal is eaasy and fast to make. It takes around 15 minutes and doesn't require a lot of ingredients.

The Italian flavours are made by adding the right seasoning and herbs

  • 300g minced beef
  • 250g penne pasta
  • 500ml passata
  1. Put on some water and boil it
  2. Cook the pasta in the boiling water for around 11 minutes
  3. Cook the minced beef in a pan
  4. once the beef is cooked, add the passata and add seasoning
  5. drain the pasta and add the sauce in the pan
